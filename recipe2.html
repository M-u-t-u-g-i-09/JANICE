Ingredients

  • For Ugali:
  • 2 cups maize flour (unga ya ugali)
  • 4 cups water
  • A pinch of salt (optional)
  • For Sukuma Wiki (collard greens):
  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • For Beef Stew:
  • 500g beef (cubed)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 green pepper (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 beef stock cube (optional)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

üç≥ Preparation Method:

  1. Place cubed beef in a pot with a little water, garlic, ginger, and salt. Simmer until tender and the water reduces.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and fry onions until soft and slightly golden.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes (and tomato paste if using). Cook until soft and saucy.
  4. Add green pepper, cooked beef, stock cube, and a little water if needed. Let it simmer and thicken.
  5. Sukuma Wiki:
  6. In a pan, heat oil and fry onions until translucent.
  7. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  8. Toss in chopped sukuma wiki and stir. Cook for 5‚Äì7 minutes until wilted but still green.
  9. Add salt and stir.
  10. UGALI
  11. In a sufuria, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  12. Slowly add maize flour while stirring with a mwiko (wooden spoon) to avoid lumps.
  13. Keep stirring until thick. Cover for 3-5 minutes to cook through.
  14. Stir again until firm. Serve hot.
